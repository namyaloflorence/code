5.2 chemical test for food substances
  1. food testing is the process by which food and drinks are taken to the laboratory to confirm the type of food components in them. Food testing is done to confirm the presence of starch , reducing sugars, proteins ,fats and vitamin C in food sample
5.3 Testing for starch
  1. Starch is a polysaccharide comprising of glucose monomers linked together. starch is insolube in water. it does not have a sweet taste and on hydrolysis, it readily converts back to monosaccharides
